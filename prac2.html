About Our Organisation & Goals

Antwi's Vendor is a wholesale-oriented company focused on supporting sustainability policies worldwide by importing slightly used and new but rejected products for customers and retailers.

Our goal is to reduce waste by giving usable products a second life while creating jobs and promoting affordable access to electronics.
